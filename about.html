About SweetArt

SweetArt bakery is established on 1998 and developed out of Sonia and Sam passion to combine their baking skills and art to bring memorable taste to special moment of people life. Sonia and Sam were graduated from bakery program from georgian college in 1998. They started their business from their home. In 2002, they opened their shop at Young street. Their business start growing very fast since they have many unique recipes that couldn't be found any where else. They are famous for the chocolate cake.
